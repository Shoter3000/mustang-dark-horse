Front End

The seventh-generation Mustang's front end is a sharp evolution of its iconic design. The tri-bar LED headlights are now more angular and aggressive, flanking a larger, more prominent grille. The sculpted hood features strong power domes, hinting at the powerful engine beneath. This design blends modern aggression with classic Mustang DNA.

Rear End

The rear of the new Mustang maintains the signature tri-bar taillights, now with a more chiseled and three-dimensional appearance. A subtle spoiler integrated into the decklid and a redesigned rear diffuser contribute to improved aerodynamics and a more planted stance. It's a modern take on a classic Mustang rear.

Interior

The seventh-generation Mustang's interior is a driver-focused cockpit blending classic muscle car heritage with cutting-edge technology. A 12.4-inch digital instrument cluster and a 13.2-inch center touchscreen, putting vital information and controls at the driver. The flat-bottom steering wheel and available Recaro seats further enhance driving experience.
